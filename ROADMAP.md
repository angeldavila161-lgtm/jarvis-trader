# StockStack Roadmap

## Mission
Build the simplest AI-powered trading workspace that helps traders make disciplined decisions through data, risk management, and repeatable processes.

---

## Version 0.1 (MVP)

- Live stock quotes
- Analyze ticker
- Risk calculator
- Dashboard
- Trade journal

---

## Version 0.2

- Watchlists
- Portfolio tracking
- AI scoring
- Charts

---

## Version 0.3

- Market scanner
- Daily opportunities
- Alerts
- Performance analytics

---

## Version 1.0

- Public beta
- User accounts
- Subscription
- Mobile support
